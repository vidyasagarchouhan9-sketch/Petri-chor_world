🎯 Goal

        
Wake up alone on the derelict station. Explore every room, gather tools and
        clearance items, hack the mainframe, and restore the station AI —
        then reach the Airlock & Docking Bay to complete your escape.

      

      

        
💡 Tips

        
Locked doors glow red and need a specific item. Crates and lockers may hold
        useful gear or just collectibles. Some rooms hide secrets — look closely.
