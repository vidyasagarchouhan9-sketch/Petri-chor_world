🎭 ACT III — THE VILLAIN'S PLAN

Deep underground, Petri-Chor reveals a giant hologram of the Maharajas' Express.

His crew watches in amazement.

"Tonight we steal the train."

Behind him stand enormous machines, robots, and anti-gravity engines.

"Why not rob a bank?" asks a henchman.

"Because trains are cooler."

Everyone agrees.
