# 🚂 Petri-Chor: The Maharaja’s Express Heist

Welcome to **Petri-Chor**, a sci-fi mystery adventure storytelling website created by **Vidyasagar** 🌌✨

---

## 📖 About the Project

**Petri-Chor: The Maharaja’s Express Heist** is an interactive web-based story that combines:
- 🚀 Science Fiction
- 🔍 Mystery
- 🎭 Adventure
- 😂 Comedy

The story follows a thrilling universe involving space science, impossible events, and the legendary Maharajas’ Express.

## 🎭 Story Structure

The story is divided into multiple acts:

- Act I — The Rain  
- Act II — The Scientist’s Journey  
- Act III — The Villain’s Plan  
- Act IV — Distraction  
- Act V — The Heist  
- ...and many more 🚂
